Spicy Tuna Salad (2 Servings)

Nutrition per serving:

  • 318 Calories
  • 19g Protein
  • 21g Fat
  • 13g Carbs

Ingredients

  • Protein: 1 can Light Tuna (drained)
  • Sauce Mix: ½ tbsp Soy Sauce, 2½ tbsp Kewpie Mayonnaise, 1½ tbsp Sriracha,
    ½ tbsp Sesame Oil, ½ tsp Black Pepper
  • Veggies: 1 cup Celery (chopped), 3 Green Onions (chopped), 1 cup
    Carrots (grated)
  • Toppings: 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds, ¼ pack Roasted Seaweed Snack (Kirkland Signature)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tuna Mix: In a bowl, combine the drained tuna with
    soy sauce, Kewpie mayo, sriracha, sesame oil, and black pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the Veggies: Stir in the chopped celery, green onions, and grated carrots.
  3. Finish & Serve: Sprinkle sesame seeds on top and serve with roasted seaweed
    snacks for wrapping or scooping.
